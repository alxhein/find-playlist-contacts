Streamline Music's playlist contact tool searches through hundreds of Spotify playlists and finds emails and instagram contacts for the playlist owners. You can then view and download a spreadsheet of all the playlists and contacts for music promotion purposes, simplifying a process that usually takes hours for independent musicians.
